About Us

Welcome to the LSE Quiz Society! We are a community of students passionate about trivia, general knowledge, and competitive quizzing.

Whether you're a casual quizzer or aspiring to compete at University Challenge level, there's a place for you here.

What We Do

  • Weekly pub quizzes
  • Inter-university competitions - (Quizbowl)
  • University Challenge tryouts and training
  • Social events and quiz nights
